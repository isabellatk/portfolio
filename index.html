Speaking & Teaching activities

I have tutored and lectured a university course about Image Processing and Analysis. In 2025 I held a talk about gamification in science communication at the Living Planet Symposium.

On primary schools, i regularly hold workshops about technology in education for teachers and students and teach the use of tablets at school.

I also contribute to science communication initiatives and outreach events focused on sustainability, innovation, and digital literacy.

My aim is to make complex spatial, scientific and environmental phenomena understandable and engaging for diverse audiences.


Further passions

Escape Game Design

Wedding Escape Room: The Secret of Conte Benedek

Designed for the wedding on a castle as a venue, the gamified experience of "Conte Benedek" sends wedding guests through different riddles focussing on solving the secret of the Conte. Through different stories and tasks, tailored to the wedding couple's story, mixed with
the venue - guests learn about the bride and groom,
woven into the story of the venue.

Personalized Scavenger Hunt

A Life's Journey: Celebrating a round Birthday

Based on experiences, hobbies, passions and characteristics of the birthday person, I developed a scavenger hunt style journey with humorous tasks. Starting from a map, tasks had to be completed to find out the next cache — and to finally gather the magic key, find the gatekeeper and enter the room with the treasure.

Magic-themed Bachelorette Adventure

The Herb Witches' Trial: Certification as a magical wife

Based on a full-day adventure including different locations and stops, the storyline tested the bride’s skills as a future wife. Creative challenges, an immersive narrative and a final magical trial awaited the bride and her friends.
